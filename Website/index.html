Background

Divra Bloxd Extention replaces the retired Divra Bloxd Userscript, has all the features of Divra Bloxd Userscript, and is approved by Bloxd.io's creator and owner Arthur Baker. This extension is still in beta but is planned to be releced to the public by the end of may as a verified Chrome Extension.
